03 : About me

Who am I

I am Liang Yixuan a secondary school student from Teck Whye Secondary School. President for their Infocomm Club, Audio Visual Aid (AVA) leader, and the favourite information technology rep (IT) for many teachers.

Why do I code

I code for self interest. Coding is a form of art, as you have to take pride in your lines of code. Making sure to follow the rule of "Do not repeat yourself" (DRY), having clean code, and making sure it readable. Futhermore, although for most of my programmes I am reinventing the wheel, but I feel that it helps me become more independant. Creating my own software allows me to utilize it for my own purposes without all the other stuff lurking in websites that hosts these programmes. It also teaches me how to read up on documentation and search on stackover flow for my answers. These reasons propel my continous interest in programming.

My vision statement

What I want to be in the future... Is a freelance programmer, which programmes to help society and solve world problems. Being a programmer is not just about making money. It is about the challenges you can overcome by empowering machines to help solve the problems. Think smart, not hard.
